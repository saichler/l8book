Why This Edition Exists

The original Layer 8 book is a technical argument. It explains why modern software becomes slow, expensive, fragile, and difficult to change, and it explains the architectural rules Layer 8 uses to prevent that outcome.

This edition keeps the same argument, but translates it for non-technical readers.

It is written for founders, CEOs, investors, executives, product leaders, non-technical operators, and anyone who needs to understand why software has become so expensive to build and maintain.

You do not need to know programming to understand this version.

The purpose of this edition is simple: explain why software organizations slow down as they grow, show that the real problem is structural, explain why AI alone does not fix it, and show how Layer 8 reframes software as an economic and organizational design problem.
